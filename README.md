🌱 AgroSense: AI-Based Crop Disease Monitoring & Management System

Crop diseases remain one of the major challenges affecting agricultural productivity and global food security. Early detection and accurate diagnosis are essential to minimize crop losses and support sustainable farming practices.

AgroSense is an intelligent crop disease detection system powered by Deep Learning. It leverages Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to automatically identify plant diseases from leaf images with high accuracy.

🚀 Key Features
🌿 Automated Disease Detection
Utilizes a CNN-based model trained on a dataset of over 38,000 images across 38 classes, covering both healthy and diseased plant leaves.
🔁 Data Augmentation
Techniques such as rotation, flipping, zooming, and brightness adjustment are applied to improve model generalization and reduce overfitting.
🧠 EfficientNetB4 Backbone
Uses EfficientNetB4, a state-of-the-art architecture, for powerful feature extraction and accurate classification.
📊 Disease Severity Prediction
Goes beyond detection by estimating the severity of the disease to help farmers understand the extent of damage.
💡 Smart Recommendation Engine
Provides actionable suggestions for prevention and control measures, enabling better decision-making.
📱 Cross-Platform Deployment
Designed for both web and mobile applications, making it accessible and practical for real-world farming use.
🌍 Impact

AgroSense empowers farmers with early disease diagnosis, enabling timely intervention and reducing crop losses. By integrating AI into agriculture, this system supports precision farming and contributes to improved productivity and sustainability.
